Work with us

At TrikeApps, we value and trust our people. We give them a tools budget and allow them to choose the tools that they prefer. We believe in self-driven professional development - we regularly send our staff to conferences and meetups - and offer career progression in both technical and people-leadership streams.

We work in an open, relaxed space in Fitzroy, Victoria, Australia with plenty of great food and coffee options nearby, and support flexible working hours. We have several developers who work remotely from locations all around the world.
