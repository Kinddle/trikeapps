Bellroy

The culmination of the work of a team of creative geniuses, shepherded by TrikeApps.

The Project

A complete e-commerce site for Bellroy, worldwide sellers of wallets and carryologists (experts in how to carry stuff). A supporting logistics platform, advertising platform, data warehousing solution and content management system.

The Results

The Bellroy webstore has been through multiple technology iterations - Shopify to Magento to a custom Ruby on Rails app. As sales have grown rapidly, so have Bellroy's requirements for enterprise systems. TrikeApps has built and continues to build systems to support Bellroy through customer purchase, fulfillment and retention.
